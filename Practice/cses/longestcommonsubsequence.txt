8️⃣ Deeper Insight (Important for You)

You’ve been thinking about DP collapsing and greedy.

Reconstruction gives you a powerful diagnostic tool:

If at every state there is exactly one optimal transition,
then the DP frontier has width 1.

That is when DP collapses to greedy.

If multiple transitions are optimal,
frontier width > 1,
greedy is unsafe.
